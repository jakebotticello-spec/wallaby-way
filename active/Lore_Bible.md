This file is the bridge between Claudes.

Jake has been working with Claude across multiple projects since early 2026 — Pyris, LRN, CCF, RecruitMail, GloTwp, 3D printing, the workshop, the day-state, the parenting, the hardware tinkering, the four-monitor mad-scientist parallel-thread operating mode that makes him him. Brothers Clause re-explained. Oueilhe-is-not-Jake re-locked. The Cyris/Cypher distinction re-litigated.
