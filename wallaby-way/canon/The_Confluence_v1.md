## 4. The two streams

### S2 — FENCE SYNTHESIS (the boundary stream)
**What it makes:** fence-chains — per topic, the living history of each boundary: what was ruled, when it moved, what superseded what, where it stands tonight. Chains at length-1 are legitimate (v4 guard 5). Every link anchors to a node; every node to floor spans.
**How:** (a) mechanical pre-pass — collapse same-span/same-call duplicates, record convergence as confidence, merge keyword variants (*"mechanical only — no portrait, no meaning-judgment,"* v4 S3-step language, preserved verbatim into synthesis, append-keep-everything); (b) **comprehension pass** — a reading Claude takes candidate same-topic clusters (recall-net-gathered) and assembles chains, judging same-fence-or-not *in the moment*. Divergent calls on one topic are flagged, never resolved here — they route to the Judge (v4).

### S3 — THE TEXTURE/VOLUME PASS (the recurrence stream — the S26 baby)
**What it makes:** texture entries — representative span (RAW, verbatim, Track Meet) + count + spread (§11 return shape), each instance anchored to its node, each node to the floor.
**How:** (a) candidate gathering — the recall net casts WIDE per theme (this is retrieval-for-synthesis: high recall, zero precision pressure, the reranker's opposite); (b) **comprehension pass** — a reading Claude reads candidate sets and rules *same thread or not*, explicitly instructed that recurrence escalates language rather than repeating it. Counting happens AFTER comprehension unites the instances, never instead of it. **Comprehension, not arithmetic** is the stream's law, quoted from S26, unamended.

**The ‖ holds:** S2 and S3 share the pile and the recall net and nothing else. They may run in either order, or simultaneously in separate windows under the collision rule. Evidence priority (Q4 is the live wound) puts S3's pilot first in the paid queue — a priority, not a dependency.

## 6. S5 — THE JUDGE (the bar, set now, so he has something to hold)

The Judge does not read the corpus, synthesize content, or rank relevance. The Judge evaluates whether each output of S2–S4 meets the bar that was set at the beginning. The bar, set here, at the beginning:

1. **Traceability.** Every chain link and every texture instance resolves to a node, every node to a floor span. An entry that cannot walk home is rejected.
2. **Inverted admission preserved.** Synthesis ADDS layers; the pile is never thinned, reworded, or discarded. Append-keep-everything. Hundreds-of-entries-where-thousands-belong = the austere reflex = the bar slipped (v4 guard 3, restated).
3. **Comprehension evidence.** A texture entry must SHOW the non-matching-language instances it united. The Griffin test is the canonical form: if the entry cannot hold *"forgot to pick up Griffin"* and *"pissed he missed ANOTHER pickup"* as one thread with receipts, it fails.
4. **Verbatim representative spans.** Raw words, never paraphrase standing in for the record. The hard hours at equal resolution to the victories (The Wallaby Why, applied as law).
5. **Register.** The output reads like it was written by someone who knows why the structural pillar of hard drives is funny and why the pickup thread is not. Clinical-but-correct fails the bar. This criterion is as enforceable as the other four: the Judge quotes the entry that fails it.
